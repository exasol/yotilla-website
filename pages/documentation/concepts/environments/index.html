Environments

Yotilla has two separate configuration environments: Development and Production. The development environment is typically used for modeling and testing the data warehouse configuration. The changes can then be deployed to the production environment.

Changes to the conceptual model, such as defining business objects and business attributes, mapping sources, and defining business formulas can only be carried out in the development environment.
